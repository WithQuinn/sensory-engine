# Release Notes: March 1, 2026

## Quinn iOS — Start a Journey + App Polish

---

## What's New

### 🗺️ Start a Journey

You can now tell Quinn where you're going — and it will suggest the places most worth experiencing there.

Two ways to get started:

**Destination mode** — type a city or neighbourhood and Quinn streams venue suggestions tailored to what draws you in. Suggestions arrive as they're discovered, so you see results immediately rather than waiting for a full list.

**Itinerary mode** — paste your existing trip notes or booking confirmation. Quinn reads them, pulls out the places, and turns them into a journey you can revisit.

Once your venues are ready, tap **Save Journey** to keep them on your device for the trip.

---

### 🔔 Permission toggles now update immediately

Previously, if you went to iOS Settings to grant Photos or Notifications access and then came back to Quinn, the toggles still showed as off — you had to leave and re-enter the screen to see them update.

That's fixed. The moment you return to Quinn from Settings, the toggles reflect your actual permission state.

---

### 🎙️ Clearer feedback on short voice clips

If you release a voice recording too quickly (under half a second), Quinn now tells you what happened:

> *"Clip too short — hold to record at least half a second."*

Previously the clip was silently discarded with no explanation.

---

## Fixes & Improvements

- **Save Journey** button is now greyed out when no venues are loaded, preventing accidental empty saves
- General responsiveness improvements when navigating back mid-search
